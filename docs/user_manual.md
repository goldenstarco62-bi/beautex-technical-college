# BTC-MS: User Manual (Manual Book)

## 📖 Welcome to Beautex Technical College MS
This manual provides instructions for the four primary roles within the system.

---

## 🔐 1. Getting Started
- **Login**: Use your official institutional email and password to access the system.
- **Change Password**: Upon your first login, or if you feel your security is compromised, go to **Profile** > **Change Password**.
- **Dashboard**: Your home screen provides a summary of your activity, recent announcements, and key statistics.

---

## 👑 2. Super Admin Guide (Total Control)

### 2.1 Managing Users
- Go to **Users** in the sidebar.
- **Create User**: Click "Add User", provide details, and assign a role (Admin, Teacher, or Student).
- **Update Role/Status**: Use the edit icons to change a user's role or deactivate their account.
- **Finance Permissions**: To give an Admin the power to **edit** financial records, find them in the user list and toggle the **Finance Editor** switch.

### 2.2 Academic Setup
- Go to **Academic Master**.
- **Departments**: Add new departments (e.g., COSMETOLOGY, FASHION).
- **Academic Periods**: Create and **Activate** terms/semesters. Only the *active* period is used for new financial entries.

### 2.3 System Maintenance
- Go to **Settings** to update the college name, logo, or contact info.
- Use **Audit Logs** to review who has made critical changes to the system.

---

## 💼 3. Admin Guide (Operational Excellence)

### 3.1 Student & Faculty Tracking
- Go to **Students** or **Faculty**.
- **Register**: Use the "Register" button to add new individuals. 
- **Bulk Import**: Use the CSV tool to register many students at once.

### 3.2 Finance & Fee Collection
- Go to **Finance**.
- **Record Payment**: 
    1. Click "Record Payment".
    2. Search and select a student.
    3. Enter the amount, method, and reference ID.
    4. Click "Authorize & Record".
- **Student Ledger**: Click on any student in the list to see their full transaction history and print receipts.

### 3.3 Announcements
- Go to **Announcements**.
- Post news, holiday notices, or exam schedules that will be visible across all portals.

---

## 👩‍🏫 4. Trainer Guide (Academic Excellence)

### 4.1 Attendance & Grading
- Go to **Attendance**: Select your class student and mark them present or absent.
- Go to **Grades**: 
    - **Single Entry**: Search for a student and update their specific grade.
    - **Batch Entry**: Select a course/batch to enter grades for the entire class at once.

### 4.2 Professional Reporting
- Go to **Reports** > **Daily Student Logs**:
    - Record the topic you covered today.
    - Add remarks for each student.
- Go to **Trainer Reports**: Submit your daily/weekly activity summary for administrative review.

---

## 🎓 5. Student Guide (Learner Success)

### 5.1 My Progress
- **Dashboard**: See your attendance percentage and latest grades.
- **Attendance**: Click the calendar icon to view your history of presence in class.

### 5.2 My Finances
- Go to **Finance**.
- View your **Total Due**, **Paid**, and **Balance**.
- Review your historical payments and download official receipts if needed.

### 5.3 Learning Materials
- Go to **Materials**.
- Download lecture notes, assignments, and study guides uploaded by your trainers.

---

## 🛠️ 6. Troubleshooting
- **Cannot Log In**: Ensure you are using the correct email. If you've forgotten your password, contact an Admin or Super Admin to reset it for you.
- **Data Not Saving**: Check your internet connection. Refresh the page and try again.
- **Page Not Loading**: Try clearing your browser cache or opening the system in an "Incognito/Private" window.
